Vulnerability Research

Our in-depth and comprehensive approach to uncovering and documenting system vulnerabilities is a major asset in cybersecurity defense. By actively researching and identifying potential threats in a system, our team anticipates exploits that hackers may leverage. This ongoing, proactive process targets a range of environments, extending to but not limited to software, firmware, hardware, and network systems. The end-goal is to identify vulnerabilities, study their impact, develop mitigations, and contribute to the overall hardening of your system. We keep you prepared for possible threat scenarios, ensuring you are always a step ahead.

Reverse Engineering

We can provide a comprehensive and deep-dive analysis of your software and hardware components. The primary goal of this service is to decipher the intricate structures, functionalities, and potential weaknesses that could be exploited by adversaries. Through a meticulous dissection of code, protocols, and algorithms, we delve into the internal workings of your systems. This process allows us to identify any hidden flaws or vulnerabilities, and also helps us to understand complex software behavior. Whether we're dealing with proprietary, custom-built, or legacy systems, we are proficient at identifying potential security threats, proposing effective solutions, and suggesting potential enhancements.

Malware Analysis

We specialise in the systematic breakdown and investigation of malware, employing advanced techniques to uncover its origin, purpose, and potential for harm. Our analysis process includes static and dynamic methodologies, providing a detailed dissection of sample code structure alongside revealing real-time interaction with system resources and potential propagation mechanisms. Our team is adept at analyzing a wide range of malicious software, from common threats to advanced persistent threats (APTs) that may lurk undetected within systems. By thoroughly understanding the intricacies of these threats, we offer insights to fortify your system defenses, tailor your response protocols, and ensure the security integrity of your environment against malicious software attacks.

Penetration Test

Predominantly manually executed to explore attack paths tailored in context of the targeted environment. Our goal is focused on complete asset take over, identification and risk assessment pertaining to leverageable vulnerabilities, integrated security controls, misconfigurations and overall implementation. We offer this service against a variety of environments.

Reconnaissance & Intelligence

We target organisations, employees and individuals to determine their level of discoverability. Our objective is to obtain information from various resources which could be leveraged for further sophisticated attacks.

Vulnerability Assessment

Execution of automated test cases in parallel with manual support accompanied by the prime objective to identify, quantify and determine the priority of vulnerabilities impacting the target.

Mobile Application Assessment

Application layer, binary analysis and local device exploitation. We offer this service across Android, iOS & UWPs.

Environment Breakout

We assess the efficiency of your locked down environment through an attempt to escape intended restrictions and escalate privileges. We have experience performing this service across; Citrix containers, restricted shell environments (rShell), restricted operating environments (SOEs) and more.

Subdomain Takeover

We seek any vulnerable DNS configuration which may allow unauthorised takeover of subdomains belonging to the target's core subnet or cloud ecosystem.

Attack Simulation

A real-world simulated cyber attack against any developed environment. These engagements are objective-orientated and our techniques unconventional to emulate apex threat actors. We incorporate attack realism and stealth tradecraft to ensure that your remediations and cyber defence use cases are built from relevant and real-world tactics, techniques and procedures (TTPs).

Configuration Review

Offered towards various systems and appliances across implemented infrastructure, as well as, network layer and application layer resources. This includes but is not limited to; firewalls, load balancers, IaaS, SaaS, PaaS platforms, Content Management Systems, Databases and more.

Secure Code Review

We offer source code analysis to evaluate, review and identify security flaws within your codebase.

Offline Computational Attacks

Cryptographic hash audits, deterministic modelling and entropy analysis.

Anti D/DoS

An architectural perspective; application, infrastructure and network related controls.

Incident Response

Experienced a breach? We could assist in attack path identification, risk evaluation of compromise, malware & persistent access (backdoor) removal.
